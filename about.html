Fractured Fairfax:About

Fractured Fairfax is a satirical web site about Fairfax County, Virginia.

Combining half-assed research, thinly-disguised plagiarism and a fascination for the trivial and mundane with a hefty dose of snarky obnoxiousness, Fractured Fairfax will tell you absolutely nothing you need to know about Fairfax County.
